## 📈 Project 1: Dynamic Sales & Profit Intelligence Dashboard
> **Status:** Completed ✅ | **Level:** Professional Analyst

### ✨ Project Vision
Is project ka main uddeshya ek factory aur retail business ke 4 saal (2014-2017) ke data ko analyze karna tha. Maine raw data ko clean karke ek aisa "Intelligence System" banaya hai jo business owners ko ye batata hai ki unka munafa (profit) kaha se aa raha hai.

### 🖼️ Dashboard Preview
<img width="2341" height="877" alt="! Alt text (imagesphoto png)" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/863255cc-a383-4b8f-b5f3-9c64fc9e8db4" />

### 🔍 Deep-Dive Insights (The "What & Why")
*   **💹 Profit Rocket:** Maine ye discover kiya ki business ka profit steadily badha hai—$21,492 se shuru hokar ye $50,684 tak pahunch gaya!
*   **📅 Seasonal Spikes:** Data analysis se pata chala ki March, September aur November mein shopping sabse zyada hoti hai. Business ko in mahino mein stock zyada rakhna chahiye.
*   **📦 Product Power:** Funnel Chart se saaf dikhta hai ki **Phones** aur **Chairs** hamare "Star Products" hain.
*   **👥 VIP Customers:** Top 5 customers ki pehchan ki gayi hai jo total profit ka ek bada hissa generate karte hain. Inhe special discounts dena business ke liye faydemand hoga.

### 🛠️ Technical Toolkit
*   **Data Cleaning:** Power Query aur Excel filters ka use karke `sales data.csv` ko process kiya.
*   **Visualizations:** Map Charts (Geographic tracking), Area Charts (Time analysis), aur Funnel Charts ka professional use.

## ☕ Project 2: Premium Coffee Shop Sales Analysis
> **Status:** Completed ✅ | **Theme:** Moody Rainy Afternoon 🌧️

### ✨ Project Vision
Ye project ek New York based coffee shop chain ke operations par focus karta hai. Maine yahan **20,510 footfall** (customers) ka data handle kiya hai taaki shop ki efficiency badhai ja sake.

### 🖼️ Dashboard Preview
<img width="1784" height="635" alt="coffee Pic " src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e332f490-6dac-47df-8e66-42b40a048cb2" />


### 🔍 Deep-Dive Insights (The "What & Why")
*   **💰 Revenue Snapshot:** Shop ne total **$96,894.48** ki sales generate ki hai.
*   **📍 Location Battle:** Astoria, Hell's Kitchen, aur Lower Manhattan ke beech comparison kiya. Astoria mein customer engagement sabse behtar mili.
*   **⏰ The Rush Hour:** Analysis dikhata hai ki subah **8:00 AM - 10:00 AM** sabse busy time hota hai. Is waqt staff ki sankhya badhani chahiye.
*   **🥐 Product Mix:** Bakery items aur Brewed coffee ka combination sabse zyada revenue deta hai.

### 🛠️ Technical Toolkit
*   **Advanced Formulas:** Data ko hours aur days mein divide karne ke liye Excel functions ka use kiya.
*   **Dynamic UX:** Month aur Day slicers add kiye hain taaki user kisi bhi specific time period ka data dekh sake.

## 👗 Project 3: Vrinda Store Annual Sales Report 2022
> **Status:** Completed ✅ | **Domain:** E-commerce & Retail

### ✨ Project Vision
Is project mein maine ek retail clothing store "Vrinda Store" ka pure saal ka data analyze kiya hai. Is dashboard ka main kaam ye batana hai ki business ko sabse zyada orders kahan se mil rahe hain aur kaunsa customer segment sabse profitable hai.

### 🖼️ Dashboard Preview
<img width="1856" height="659" alt="Vrinda Store Data Analysis" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7bbbffb6-e744-4a27-8b4d-d562a7deba14" />


### 🔍 Deep-Dive Insights:
*   **📈 Sales vs Orders:** Pure saal mein March ka mahina sabse peak par raha, jisme orders aur sales dono ne high jump liya.
*   **👩 Gender-Based Sales:** Dashboard se saaf dikhta hai ki **64% sales Women** segment se aa rahi hain.
*   **🌍 Geographic Reach:** **Maharashtra (2.99M)** aur **Karnataka (2.65M)** hamare top performing states hain.
*   **🛒 Channel Performance:** Sabse zyada orders **Amazon (35%)** aur **Flipkart (22%)** se aa rahe hain, jo dikhata hai ki online marketplaces ka business par kitna bada asar hai.
*   **📊 Order Status:** **92% orders successful deliver** huye hain, jo ek bahut achha delivery rate hai.

### 🛠️ Technical Toolkit:
*   **Pivot Charts & Slicers:** Dynamic filtering ke liye (Month, Channel, Category).
*   **Data Modeling:** Age aur Gender groups ko segregate karke analysis kiya gaya.
