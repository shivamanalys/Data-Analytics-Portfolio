### 📝 Project Description (English)
In this project, I analyzed a large sales dataset (2014-2017) to find hidden patterns in business performance.

**What I did in this project:**
1.  **Data Processing:** I cleaned the raw `sales data.csv` file using Excel to ensure all dates and numbers were accurate.
2.  **Trend Analysis:** I identified that sales usually increase during the end of the year (September to November).
3.  **Profit Tracking:** I created a "Profit Gained Over Time" chart which shows the business growing from $21k to over $50k in profit.
4.  **Category Focus:** I used a **Funnel Chart** to show that Technology and Furniture are the main drivers of the business.
5.  **Customer Insights:** I identified the Top 5 customers who are most profitable for the company using a Pie Chart.

### 🛠️ Tools Used:
*   **Microsoft Excel:** Pivot Tables, Slicers, and Advanced Data Visualization.
*   **Dataset:** Sales Data CSV.

### 📂 How to use this folder:
*   Open `Sales_Dashboard.xlsx` to interact with the slicers and filters.
*   The raw data is available in `sales data.csv`.
